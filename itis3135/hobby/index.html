What is FFXIV?

Final Fantasy XIV is a massive online multiplayer RPG where players explore the world of Eorzea, complete quests, dungeons, raids, or just sit around talking to other players.

As like most Final Fantasy games, the story is what keeps you coming back, and it's not different here. Many players even decide not to interact with the MMO aspect and play the game solely for how rich the story and world are.

The game features multiple jobs/classes that you can play all on a single character. Where most other games would require you to make a new character for each class.
