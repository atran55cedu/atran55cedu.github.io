What is FFXIV?

Final Fantasy XIV is a massive online multiplayer RPG where players explore the world of Eorzea, complete quests, dungeons, raids, or take part of the abundance of social aspects.

As with most Final Fantasy games, the story is what keeps you coming back, and it's not different here. Many players even decide not to interact with the MMO aspect of meeting other players and play the game solely for how rich the story and world are.

FFXIV also features an unrestricted free trial. You can keep playing until you purchase a subscription. Realistically, that means hundreds or thousands of hours before you ever need to spend money to continue the game.

The game features multiple jobs/classes that you can play all on a single character. Where most other games would require you to make a new character for each class.

How to Get Started

Download the game from either the official Square Enix site or Steam.

Understandably, you want a game to be seamless - purchase, then play; however, you should probably adjust some settings to not get overwhelmed.

Some suggestions would be to:

  • Change the HUD/User Interface to reduce visual clutter.
  • Adjust keybindings to make it easier to use actions and interact with the game
  • Choose any class that catches your eye. The game allows you to unlock other classes very early on.

Why I Love FFXIV

I originally started playing because my ex had a lot of interest in it. I was off and on with the game for a while, but, as I progressed, I began enjoying the simple combat and got hooked on the story.

FFXIV stands out for its emotional storylines, and one of, if not, the kindest community.
What continues to draw me to the game mainly is that it's currently the best MMO in terms of all around polish. There just isn't another game that can compare. Until one does come out, I will be sticking with this.
